About me

Hi! I’m Almasur Antor but you can call me Almas. Growing up I didn’t really have much access to video games or anything of that sort. My parents always pushed me towards sports, don’t get me wrong, I love sports till this day. But once I was first introduced to the gaming world I was instantly obsessed. Games helped me get through a lot, gaming was a doorway to escape reality and helped me ease my mind from negativity and stress. My parents didn’t like me playing games though, in fact I never owned a proper console to play games. I used to play games on my laggy laptop but luckily for me, I was recently finally able to get a gaming pc.
